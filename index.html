A Letter For You

June 17, 2026

Happy Birthday, Ayo Mi 🥹💕🎂✨

Hey Love,

I don't even know how to start this without my chest tightening... but here it goes. Today is the day heaven sent you to earth, and I get to love you in real time.

Sometimes I look at you and wonder if you truly understand what you mean to me. Not the simple version, not the "I love you" people throw around, but the kind that changed the way my heart beats, the way my days feel, the way my world moves.

You came into my life quietly, but somehow you became the loudest part of my heart. And lately, I've been carrying this fear the fear of losing you, the fear of waking up one day and realizing I was just another passing chapter in your book while you became the whole story in mine.

So on your birthday, Ayo mi, I pray:

You wrap her in a peace that makes no sense the kind that quiets storms before they start.

I pray You heal every place in her that still aches from yesterday. Every disappointment, every silent tear, every prayer she thought You didn't hear let this new year answer them one by one.

Cover her health, God. From the crown of her head to the soles of her feet. Let her body be strong, her mind be sound, and her spirit be full. No emergency will come near her. No sudden fear will break her.

Go before her and make every crooked path straight. Let doors open that no man can shut. Let rooms remember her name even when she's not in them. Let favor speak for her in places her voice can't reach.

Protect her from people who pretend. From love that confuses. From friends who compete. Send her destiny helpers people who will water what You planted in her.

Give her joy that returns in the morning. Give her sleep that actually rests her. Give her ideas that turn into income, and dreams that turn into reality.

And God, if she ever doubts herself, remind her: She is fearfully and wonderfully made. She is chosen. She is covered.

I know I'm not perfect. I know I've made mistakes, spoken wrong sometimes, failed to show you how much I need you. But the truth is, I've never cared about someone the way I care about you. It scares me how much power you have over my happiness, but it also warms me, because it means I finally found someone worth that risk.

You're not just my girlfriend.

You're the person I want to tell my good news to, and the person I want to run to when I'm breaking inside.

You're the calm in my storms, even when you don't realize it. You're the answered prayer I didn't have words for yet.

I wish you a year so kind it softens the hard parts of your story. I wish you mornings that don't start with anxiety. I wish you nights that end with gratitude.

I wish you laughter that comes from your belly the kind that makes you forget to be strong for a minute. I wish you wins that feel effortless because grace is carrying you.

I wish you clarity. When life gets loud, may you still hear God whisper, "This is the way." I wish you courage to leave tables where you're not being fed. I wish you discipline to protect your peace like it's gold.

May this be the year you stop apologizing for taking up space. The year your bank account matches your worth. The year your smile comes back permanently.

May you look in the mirror at 11:11pm and finally believe what I've been saying all along: You are enough. You always were.

And if you ever felt even once that you are not enough, that you don't matter, I want you to read these words again and again: You are my heart in human form. You are the reason I believe love isn't a scam.

I don't want anyone else. I don't even look at the world the same since you walked into mine.

And if you ever cried thinking I don't love you, swear those are the tears I'd cross oceans to wipe away. Those are the nights I'd lose sleep just to remind you that you're safe with me.

I'm here.

I choose you.

Not because I'm lonely, not because I need someone, but because your soul feels like home, and I don't want to lose the only place I've ever truly felt safe the place I feel more comfortable, as the name implies, comfort.

So today, I don't just celebrate your age. I celebrate your existence.

I celebrate your scars and your survival. I celebrate the little girl you were and the woman you're becoming.

Thank you for being born, Ayo mi.

Thank you for letting me love you.

Thank you for being the reason my heart learned how to beat out loud.

May this new age find you softer, stronger, richer, and more in love with yourself. May it find us still choosing each other, still fighting for this, still winning.

Happy Birthday, baby. I love you past words, past time, past this life. 🎂✨💕

Forever Yours ❤️

One More Thing...

Before you go, I want you to know something...

You are the most amazing person I have ever known.

Your birthday is not just a celebration of the day you were born, but a celebration of everything you are - your beautiful heart, your radiant soul, and your incredible spirit.

I hope this small forward to creating countless more beautiful memories together. Happy Birthday, my love.
